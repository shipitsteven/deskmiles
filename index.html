DeskMiles Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Overview

DeskMiles is a commute and parking tracker that runs entirely on your device. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, or transmit your data anywhere.

Data We Access

Precise Location: DeskMiles uses your device's location services to detect when you arrive at or leave your workplace. This enables automatic commute tracking and parking reminders.

How Data Is Stored

All data — including commute history, parking spots, and settings — is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if iCloud is enabled). No data is sent to external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. Period.

Third-Party Services

DeskMiles does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Your Control

You can revoke location access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services. You can delete all app data by removing the app from your device.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at deskmiles@steeeve.work
